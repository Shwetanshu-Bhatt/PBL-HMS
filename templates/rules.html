Hostel Rules and Regulations

Welcome, {{ student.name if student else "Student" }}

General Conduct

  • All residents must maintain discipline and decorum within hostel premises.
  • Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate expulsion.
  • Silence must be maintained in study areas and corridors after 10 PM.

Room and Cleanliness

  • Rooms must be kept clean and tidy at all times.
  • Students are responsible for maintaining furniture and electrical fittings in their rooms.
  • Cooking inside rooms is not allowed unless permitted by hostel authorities.

Attendance and Visitors

  • Residents must return to the hostel before the designated curfew time (10 PM).
  • Visitors are allowed only during visiting hours and must sign in at the hostel gate.
  • Overnight stays of outsiders are strictly prohibited.

Prohibited Activities

  • Smoking, alcohol consumption, or drug use inside hostel premises is strictly forbidden.
  • No loud music or parties are allowed without prior permission.
  • Damage to hostel property will result in fines or disciplinary action.

Disciplinary Actions

  • Violations of hostel rules may result in suspension, expulsion, or fines.
  • Repeated misconduct will be reported to university authorities.

These rules are designed to ensure a safe, clean, and respectful living environment for all residents. Please read and follow them carefully.
